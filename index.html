Everything About Me!

I graduated fall of 2016 from Northern Kentucky University with a BS in Business Administration. After graduation I moved to Dallas, Texas, and started looking for jobs. So, I set my eyes on some a pathway into the industry. I soon found that web development was in demand, and was a great way to start learning to code.
